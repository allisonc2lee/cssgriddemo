Easy Layout System

Look at the example below:

It is well known that CSS Grid is a two dimensional layout system. And it is good for building an overall container, since you can place your content in both X and Y directions(like the supports section in the right side), you have more freedom to control your grid items by changing the column and row line numbers.

There are a few situations that you might want to apply Grid System:
  • Create the overall layout, and then place grid items inside the Grid.
  • Work on your content in both directions.
  • Control Grid items' positioning
